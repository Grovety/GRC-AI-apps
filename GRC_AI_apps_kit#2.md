# GRC AI apps kit#2

## Rhythm PIN
- Name: AIRhythmKey
- Messages:
  - correct
  - incorrect
  - unlocked
  - locked
- After entering the correct rhythm key, a high signal is sent to pin `GPIO_NUM_14` on ESP32S3 part which is connected to `GPIO_NUM_2` on ESP32C3 part.
